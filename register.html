Early Bird Rates and Registration

Registrations provide access to all conference sessions, including those in the ALICE special track. Please note that the student rate is only for full-time students (proof of status required). The Early Bird Rates will be available through February 28, 2022. Save up to $100 by registering early! Questions? Please feel free to contact us at info@learningideasconf.org.



RegisterCancellation Policy:


For cancellations through January 31, 2022, a refund of 75% of the fee will be issued.

For cancellations between April 1 and May 7, 2022, a refund of 25% of the fee will be issued.


For cancellations after May 7, 2022, no refund will be issued.
